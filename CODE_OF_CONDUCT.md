# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a welcoming, professional, and respectful environment for everyone participating in this project.

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

* Be respectful and professional.
* Provide constructive feedback.
* Share knowledge openly.
* Support learning and collaboration.
* Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors will not be tolerated:

* Harassment or discrimination.
* Personal attacks or insults.
* Offensive language.
* Intimidation or threats.
* Sharing confidential or sensitive information without authorization.
* Spam or self-promotional content unrelated to the project.

## Community Guidelines

* Focus discussions on technical topics.
* Keep feedback objective and constructive.
* Help maintain a positive learning environment.
* Respect maintainers' decisions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributors that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Our Goal

To build a collaborative community where engineers can learn, contribute, and share enterprise AWS and DevOps knowledge in a respectful environment.
